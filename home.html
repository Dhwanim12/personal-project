Criterion A

Students should state a clear and consice learning goal, based on a personal interest. They should also determine a product goal and success criteria to assess that product. Students should collect evidence of short-term and long-term planning.

Criterion B

Students need to explain what ATL skills were used to achieve their learning goal and their product goal. Evidence needs to be shown for each of the skills.

Criterion C

Students need to reflect on their final product, and the process of developing the product. They also need to create a KWL table and learner profile attributes table. They also need to grade their product according to the success criterion previously created, and justify the grade with surveys.
