Time Managment and Stress: Advice from your Seniors and Mentors



The Personal Project is an extremely stressful time for students with students stating that they are the “two words that evoke fear in any IB student”. Additionally, 75% of students say that they are always facing high levels of stress and anxiety. But with careful planning, time managment techniques, and discipline, you can avoid this by applying the tips stated in the blog post below.



Balance

One of the biggest challenges of time management is finding the balance between productivity and rest. It's essential to take breaks regularly to avoid burnout and maintain your mental and physical health. It's also essential to avoid multitasking, as it can lead to decreased productivity and increased stress.



Ask for Help

Asking for help is an essential part of completing any project, including a personal project. When you ask for help, you open yourself up to new perspectives, ideas, and insights that can help you improve your work and overcome challenges that you may not have been able to solve on your own. One of the primary benefits of asking for help is that it can save you time and energy. When you ask for help, you can get answers and solutions more quickly, which can help you stay on track and meet your deadlines. It also allows you to delegate tasks that you may not have the skills or time to complete, allowing you to focus on the areas where you excel. Another benefit of asking for help is that it can help you build relationships and connect with others. When you work on a personal project, it can be easy to become isolated and lose sight of the bigger picture. By reaching out to others and seeking their help, you can build new connections and gain valuable insights that can help you grow both personally and professionally.



Stay focused

Online focus tools can be immensely helpful to stay on task and stop procrastination.

Forest: This app helps you stay focused by planting a virtual tree on your phone. The tree grows while you work, but if you leave the app, the tree dies. This encourages you to stay focused and avoid distractions.

Focus@Will: This is a music app that provides music specifically designed to help you focus. The app uses neuroscience to create music that increases your attention span and productivity.

RescueTime: This is an app that tracks how you spend your time on your computer or phone. It provides detailed reports on how much time you spend on different websites or apps, and can help you identify areas where you are wasting time.



Organization

To stay organized, used this Notion template which allows you to input important dates for your project, visualize them in a calender, add meeting notes, and more.



Meeting Deadlines

Meeting deadlines is an essential aspect of project management. It helps to ensure that tasks are completed on time, resources are utilized efficiently, and the project is delivered within the specified timeframe. Deadlines are also a critical aspect of effective time management. Following deadlines strictly can help you stay focused on the project's goals and objectives. When you have a clear deadline in mind, you are more likely to prioritize your tasks and allocate your time and resources accordingly. This can help you to avoid distractions and stay focused on the task at hand. Furthermore, by breaking down the project into smaller, more manageable tasks with specific deadlines, you can better manage your workload and ensure that you are making steady progress towards the project's completion.

Remember that you are not alone and that you have the support from your school community, as well as online resources such as Padlet and Notion to collaborate and solve prroblems together.
