Sustainable Personal Project Ideas



The MYP Personal Project is a significant part of the Middle Years Programme. It is an independent project that allows students to explore their passions, develop their creativity, and apply their learning to their personal interests. With the world facing significant challenges related to globalization and sustainability, it is essential for MYP students to develop sustainable projects that align with the Global Context of Globalization and Sustainability. In this blog post, we will discuss different Sustainable Personal Project ideas.



Sustainable Gardening Project

Students can develop a sustainable gardening project that focuses on growing organic fruits and vegetables, which they then use to cook healthy recipes and upload on social media. The ATL skill being used in this project is research, where students can research different types of organic gardening techniques, learning about the benefits of eating locally grown produce, and designing and implementing a garden that is environmentally sustainable, through media literacy and information literacy.



Renewable Energy E-book

A renewable energy project can involve students researching different types of renewable energy sources and their impact on the environment. Based on their research, they can write a 'Sustainable Living' E-book for teenagers, where different ways of incoporating renewable energy into our daily lives are summarized.



Sustainable Fashion Project

A sustainable fashion project can focus on the impact of the fashion industry on the environment and ways to reduce that impact. Students can design and create sustainable clothing or accessories using environmentally friendly materials and production methods, while learning new skills such as stiching, sewing, and fashion designing.



Reducing Waste in your community

Students can design and implement a waste reduction project that focuses on reducing waste in their local or school community. This project could involve researching different types of waste reduction strategies, such as composting, recycling, and waste reduction campaigns, and implementing these strategies in their community or school, then discussing your experience in a blog or Youtube video.



In conclusion, these are some of the many Personal Project ideas that will not only help you create a wonderful report, but also make a positive impact on your community and for the people around you. The project is a unique opportunity for students to explore their passions while also addressing real-world issues related to globalization and sustainability.
